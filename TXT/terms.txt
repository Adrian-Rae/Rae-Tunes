Tune Room ZA (Ltd.) is an academic research venture into the function of various hypertext and script based elements, external APIs and their handling and
database manipulation. It is a non-profit venture, and is adminsitered solely by through the University of Pretoria.

As such, Tune Room is heavily dependent on the user data passed in during client-side exchanges. This includes requests made by specific users, login metadata such
as usage sessions, and access to user names and contact details. However, we ensure the confidentiality of all passwords, which are hash-encrypted and are not visible
in plaintext form.
